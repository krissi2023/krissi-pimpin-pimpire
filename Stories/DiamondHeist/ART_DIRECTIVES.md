# The Pimpire Chronicles: Art Directives
**Version 1.0 - Character Design Bible**

This document provides detailed visual specifications for all main characters to ensure consistency across all episodes and artists.

---

## DIAMOND (The Player)

### Basic Profile
- **Age:** Late 20s
- **Height:** 5'7" (athletic build)
- **Ethnicity:** Ambiguous (can be interpreted as mixed, allowing broad representation)
- **Key Trait:** Dual personality - switches between "soft elegant" and "street tactical"

### Face & Hair
**Hair:**
- Long, voluminous curls that cascade past shoulders
- Color: Deep brown with subtle honey highlights
- Style: When "elegant mode" - soft romantic curls. When "heist mode" - pulled back in tactical ponytail with loose strands framing face
- Texture: Bouncy, full-bodied, moves dramatically

**Face:**
- Large expressive eyes with long lashes
- High cheekbones
- Full lips with signature smirk (one corner slightly raised)
- Dimples when she smiles innocently
- Sharp, calculating gaze when in "thief mode"

**Expressions:**
- **Innocent Mode:** Wide eyes, soft smile, head tilted slightly, bashful
- **Thief Mode:** Narrowed eyes, confident smirk, eyebrows raised, predatory
- **Signature Look:** The "wink over the shoulder" - breaks fourth wall

### Outfit: Elegant Mode (Episodes 1 & 4)
**Evening Gown:**
- Pastel color (lavender or soft pink recommended)
- Flowing, floor-length with strategic tear-away points
- Off-shoulder or strapless design
- Pearl necklace (doubles as grapple line)
- Elbow-length gloves (satin)
- Delicate heels (that she removes for sneaking)

**Accessories:**
- Small clutch purse (contains tools disguised as makeup)
- Diamond earrings (real diamonds, obviously)
- Monocle/compact mirror (actually a tech device)

### Outfit: Tactical Mode (Episodes 2-3)
**After dress is torn:**
- Same dress but hem ripped off to mid-thigh for movement
- One shoulder strap torn
- Barefoot or combat-style flats
- Exposed garter with tools
- Hair transitions to ponytail

**Gear Visible:**
- Fingerless gloves (tactical, with grip pads)
- Small tech devices strapped to thigh
- Phone in hand (for hacking)
- Torn dress shows athletic physique underneath

### Color Palette
- **Skin Tone:** Warm medium brown with golden undertones
- **Primary Colors:** Soft pinks, purples, gold accents
- **Thief Mode Colors:** Black, silver, deep purple
- **Signature Color:** Rose gold (appears in all her scenes)

### Body Language
- **Elegant Mode:** Graceful, delicate hand gestures, head tilts, non-threatening posture
- **Thief Mode:** Confident stride, powerful poses, fluid movement like a dancer/fighter hybrid
- **Signature Move:** The "nail check" - examines nails while being condescending

## KING PIMPIN' (The Game Master)

### Basic Profile
- **Age:** Early-to-mid 30s
- **Height:** 6'1" (commanding presence)
- **Build:** Athletic/fit but not bulky (suit-wearing businessman physique)
- **Key Trait:** Always the smartest person in the room, never loses composure

### Face & Hair
**Hair:**
- Short, clean fade haircut
- Well-groomed, shaped up perfectly
- Color: Black with subtle gray streak on one temple (adds sophistication)

**Face:**
- Strong jawline
- Trimmed beard/goatee (clean, sharp lines)
- Intense eyes behind sunglasses (rarely shows full eyes)
- Confident, knowing smile (he's always in control)

**Expressions:**
- **Default:** Cool, unbothered, slight smirk
- **Impressed:** Eyebrows raised, genuine smile (rare)
- **Focused:** Leans forward, removes sunglasses, intense stare
- **Signature Look:** The "I knew you'd do that" smirk

### Outfit: Business Mode (Episodes 1 & 4)
**The Suit:**
- Three-piece suit (jacket, vest, pants)
- Color: Dark purple or midnight blue (not black)
- Perfectly tailored, custom-fit
- Gold cufflinks (Pimpire logo)
- Pocket square (gold or white)
- Expensive watch (visible on left wrist)

**Shirt & Tie:**
- Crisp white or light lavender shirt
- Gold or purple silk tie
- Top button always closed

**Shoes:**
- Polished dress shoes (oxfords or loafers)
- Color: Black or burgundy
- Mirror shine

**Accessories:**
- Signature sunglasses (gold-rimmed aviators or rectangular frames)
- Rings (one or two, subtle but expensive)
- Tech watch on left wrist (holographic display)

### Outfit: Control Room Mode (Episodes 5-6)
**Casual Executive:**
- Suit jacket removed, draped over chair
- Vest still on, shirt sleeves rolled to elbows
- Tie loosened
- More relaxed but still powerful

### Outfit: Chase Mode (Episode 6, Page 3)
**Action Ready:**
- Leather jacket (black or dark purple with gold accents)
- Dark fitted t-shirt or turtleneck underneath
- Same dress pants or tactical pants
- Sunglasses on
- Ready to move

### Color Palette
- **Skin Tone:** Deep brown with cool undertones
- **Primary Colors:** Purple, gold, black, white
- **Signature Color:** Royal purple with gold trim

### Body Language
- **Default:** Relaxed, leaning back, hands steepled
- **Analyzing:** Leans forward, touches chin, watches intensely
- **Impressed:** Stands up, slight smile, nods approval
- **Authority:** Full height, hands behind back, commanding presence

### Tech Aesthetic
**In Episodes 4-6, his POV includes:**
- Heads-up display visible to reader (Iron Man/Terminator style)
- Data boxes labeling people
- Heart rate monitors
- Threat assessment readouts
- Color: Neon gold/yellow interface

## YAGO (The Digital Sidekick)

### Basic Profile (Reality Mode - Episodes 1-6)
- **Form:** Holographic avatar, not fully physical
- **Size:** Small, appears on screens/floating near King
- **Key Trait:** Anxious, calculating, loyal but cowardly

### Appearance: Hologram Form
**Design:**
- Simplified humanoid shape (like a stylized emoji/avatar)
- Made of light particles, slightly transparent
- Color: Neon cyan blue with yellow accents
- Facial features: Large eyes, expressive eyebrows, simple mouth

**Expressions:**
- **Default:** Nervous smile, eyes darting
- **Alert:** Eyes wide, exclamation marks appear
- **Panic:** Pixelated/glitching, vibrating
- **Crashed:** X_X eyes, smoking, fragmented

**Visual Effects:**
- Flickers slightly when talking
- Leaves light trails when moving
- Glitches when stressed
- Data code visible inside transparent body

### Appearance: Digital World Form (Bonus Story ONLY)
**Full Body Design:**
- Humanoid, 5'9", athletic but not muscular
- Made of glowing polygons/wireframes
- Neon blue body with gold circuit patterns
- Digital sunglasses (matrix-style)
- Flowing cape made of light (like Doctor Strange's cape but neon)

**Face (Digital World):**
- More detailed than hologram form
- Confident expression (thinks he's a hero here)
- Glowing eyes behind sunglasses
- Heroic jawline

**Outfit (Digital World):**
- Superhero-style suit (think Tron meets superhero)
- Glowing chest emblem (Pimpire logo)
- Tech gauntlets on forearms
- Boots with light trails

**Weapons/Tools:**
- Ban Hammer: Giant glowing hammer (looks like judge's gavel + Thor's hammer)
- Firewall Shield: Circular shield made of hexagonal code
- Antivirus Sword: Glowing blade that deletes code

**Color Palette (Digital World):**
- Primary: Neon cyan (#00FFFF)
- Secondary: Electric gold (#FFD700)
- Accents: Hot pink (when infected), matrix green (when analyzing)
- Environment: Purple and blue neon grid

### Body Language
- **Hologram Mode:** Floaty, nervous energy, gestures wildly
- **Digital Hero Mode:** Confident superhero poses, cape billowing
- **Glitched Mode:** Stuttering movement, pixelated, corrupted

## ENVIRONMENT ART DIRECTION

### Real World (Episodes 1-3, Sterling's Penthouse)
**Style:** Modern luxury with tech elements

**Color Palette:**
- Whites, grays, chrome
- Accent lighting: Blue and purple
- Warning lights: Red
- Wealth indicators: Gold trim everywhere

**Key Locations:**
- **Party Hall:** Crystal chandeliers, floor-to-ceiling windows showing city, marble floors
- **Vault Entrance:** Steel, digital keypads, slot machine door
- **Laser Hallway:** Glass floor over water, holographic fish projections
- **Card Floor:** Giant playing cards, neon edges, pressure sensors visible
- **Garage:** Exotic cars, lowriders, purple convertible in spotlight

### Control Room (Episodes 4-6, King's POV)
**Style:** High-tech command center

**Setup:**
- Massive curved screens showing multiple feeds
- Gaming chair (executive style with controls)
- Neon blue/purple lighting
- Floating holographic displays
- City view through windows behind screens

**HUD Elements (visible to readers):**
- Threat assessment boxes (color-coded)
- Heart rate monitors
- Biometric scanners
- Security camera feeds
- Gold/yellow interface color

### Digital World (Yago Bonus Story ONLY)
**Style:** TRON meets The Matrix

**Elements:**
- Infinite grid floor (glowing lines)
- Floating platforms (hexagonal)
- Binary code raining down
- Data streams flowing like rivers
- No sky - just infinite digital space

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Neon cyan and electric purple
- Data: Matrix green (0s and 1s)
- Warnings: Red with klaxons
- Malware: Hot pink with skull icons

**Lighting:**
- Everything self-illuminates
- Lens flares everywhere
- Light trails behind moving objects
- Glow effects on all characters

## PANEL COMPOSITION GUIDELINES

### Action Sequences
- Use motion blur for speed
- Dynamic angles (dutch angles for chaos)
- Speed lines for impact
- Sound effects integrated into art (not just text boxes)

### Dialogue-Heavy Scenes
- Clear sight lines between speakers
- Expressive faces take priority
- Background can be simplified for focus

### "Perspective Shift" Panels
When showing same scene from different POV:
- Keep layout similar but change colors/lighting
- Add tech overlays for King's POV
- Keep Diamond's POV "cleaner" visually

### Fourth Wall Breaks
- Character looks directly at reader
- Can show panel borders breaking
- Speech bubbles can point "out" of page

---

## PAGE LAYOUT STANDARDS

### Standard Comic Format
- **Size:** 600 x 900 pixels for digital (2:3 ratio)
- **Readable on mobile:** Yes (vertical scroll)
- **Panels per page:** 4-5 average
- **Gutter space:** 10-15px between panels

### Special Pages
- **Title Pages:** Full-bleed art, title overlaid
- **Splash Pages:** 1-2 per episode for dramatic moments
- **Fourth Wall Pages:** Can break standard panel grid

## ARTIST NOTES

### What Makes This Series Unique
1. **Dual Narratives:** Same events, different perspectives - art must show this
2. **Personality Shifts:** Diamond changes modes - must be visually obvious
3. **Tech Integration:** King's POV shows data overlays - like Iron Man's HUD
4. **Genre Blending:** Heist + Romance + Comedy + Tech + Arcade games
5. **Digital World:** Yago bonus story is completely different art style

### Artistic Freedom
Artists are encouraged to:
- Add background details that enhance the "arcade/gaming" theme
- Include Easter eggs referencing real casino games
- Experiment with panel layouts for action sequences
- Add flourishes to Diamond's movements (she's a dancer at heart)
- Make King's tech look cutting-edge and cool

### Must Stay Consistent
- Character proportions and facial features
- Color palettes for each character
- Outfit details (accessories matter)
- Logo placement and branding
- Digital world visual rules (Yago bonus)
